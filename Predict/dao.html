Traditional Prediction

Centralization, the platform manipulates issues and results

Limitations, limited to simple price predictions, or certain event types

Only enables prediction without communications

Lack of liquidity in orderbook

The operating experience is complicated and unfriendly

Only support one Blockchain network

Spontaneous prediction

The user initiates a prediction issue, and the initiator can get a certain percentage of prediction rewards, prompting Users spontaneously initiate a wide range of interesting topics and promote them to attract more people’s attention

Multiple benefits

In addition to predicting correct rewards, users also predict mining rewards, staking Rewards, forecast leaderboard rewards, liquidity provision and mining rewards, etc. In addition,There are additional rewards for predicting topic sponsors and community nodes.

Community Autonomy

Any user can become a node by staking a certain governance currency. The governance node uploads the results through the oracle machine and accepts the whole network supervision, and part of the platform revenue is shared.

Asset cross-chain

Based on Polkadot's cross-chain agreement, the platform will support the participation of cross-chain assets, condense the resources and energy of each chain, and facilitate the participation of more users. It will become an independent parachain in the future

Social System

The platform will not only be a cold forecast, but also a enthusiastic way of socializing. Users will be able to discuss topics through predictions, and there will be a forum-style communication system with social functions such as friends and group chats.

PFMM

Refer to AMM's new type of fixed product automated market maker, which automatically adjusts and predicts the value of tokens, no longer requires in-depth support of thin orders and greatly increases liquidity

The governance token of Predict Dao prediction market is called POT for short. The total amount is 21 million and no additional issuance is allowed. POT will be used to participate in the governance of the prediction market, including but not limited to voting for initial proposals, staking to become nodes, reporting nodes, voting for platform governance, etc.
